Ant

The most distinguishing trait of ant behavior is sociability. Ants do not act individually. Instead, they behave according to the needs of the colony in roles dictated by the caste or job class, such as worker or soldier into which they are born. The major social unit is the colony, which forms a nest.

Neuropetera

Neuropterans are soft-bodied insects with relatively few specialized features. They have large lateral compound eyes, and may or may not also have ocelli. Their mouthparts have strong mandibles suitable for chewing, and lack the various adaptations found in most other endopterygote insect groups.
